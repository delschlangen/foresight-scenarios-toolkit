## Scenario Quality Checklist

Before finalizing, verify each scenario is:

- [ ] **Plausible** — Could actually happen given current trends
- [ ] **Internally consistent** — Logic holds together, no contradictions
- [ ] **Differentiated** — Meaningfully different from other scenarios
- [ ] **Challenging** — Forces new thinking, not business-as-usual
- [ ] **Relevant** — Directly addresses the focal question

Common problems to avoid:
- ❌ "Good future" vs "Bad future" framing
- ❌ Treating scenarios as predictions
- ❌ Science fiction (must be plausible)
- ❌ Scenarios that are too similar to each other
